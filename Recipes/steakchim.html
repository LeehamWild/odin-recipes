Steak Chimichurri

Description

Sunday 22nd May 2022 we fancied something different to our usual sunday roast. I had never seen or used chimichurri before, but this was an absolutely outstanding flavour on my steak!

Ingredients

  • Steak of your choice
  • Parsley
  • corriander
  • Chilli
  • lime
  • salt flakes
  • oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Prep your steak as you usually would. I like to add salt into my steak and rub this in before cooking.
  2. Once this is seared, add butter, garlic and rosemary to the pan and base.
  3. Once this step is done, allow your steak to rest for 10 minutes. This will allow the steak to cook through
  4. once done, add your chimichurri ingredients above together in a mixing bowl and place this on top of your steak. I did make an onion load with mine, which was also great.
  5. ENJOY!
