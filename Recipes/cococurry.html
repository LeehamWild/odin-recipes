Chickpea and Coconut Curry

Description

Tonight I made this amazing chickpea, lentil and coconut curry that I saw on TikTok. Having seen this, I just had to make it. It was a great tasting curry that I will be making again and again!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • red lentils
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • spinach
  • red chilli
  • red onion
  • spices

Steps

  1. Firstly, chop up red chilli and red onion. Once chopped up, fry this off in an oil of your choice.
  2. Once fried off, add in the coconut milk
  3. Once this has become a sauce, add in the chickpeas and lentils. Allow to simmer for 20 minutes or so.
  4. once simmered, add in the spinach and allow this to wilt down to a consistency you are happy to eat.
  5. ENJOY!
